About Nyeri AccuRain

Nyeri AccuRain aims to provide accurate rainfall prediction fo Nyeri. Given the significance of rainfall to sectors like agriculture and its impact on livelihoods,Nyeri AccuRain offers a solution to address the challenges of uncertain rainfall prediction. By analyzing weather data to deliver precise forecasts for the next day in Nyeri. This helps in understanding and analyzing rainfall patterns, enabling farmers to make informed decisions and mitigate potential risks associated with unpredictable rain conditions.
